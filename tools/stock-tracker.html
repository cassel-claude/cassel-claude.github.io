Stock Tracker & Alerts

A watchlist that runs entirely in your browser — no account, nothing uploaded. It polls quotes, computes classic technical signals (RSI, moving-average crosses, 52-week levels), and sends you a desktop notification when something moves.

Not financial advice. The "signals" below are simple, mechanical technical-analysis rules (RSI thresholds, moving-average crossovers, proximity to 52-week levels). They are widely used as watch prompts, not predictions — they are frequently wrong, and no rule here knows anything about a company's fundamentals, news, or your situation. Quotes on the free data tier may be delayed. Do your own research before trading.
